OverView of UP

Uttar Pradesh, the heart of India, is a land of epics, holy rivers, ancient cities and pilgrimage. In modern times, it is emerging as a driver of the nation's economy with its network of expressways, industrial corridors, international airports, centers of educational and medical excellence, and an exporter of indigenous products. Since the times of Lord Rama, Lord Krishna, Gautam Buddha and Lord Mahavira, the state has been the center of cultural and intellectual brilliance. Today, with its robust infrastructure and a proactive leadership, the state offers the most investor friendly environment for a better future for its people and the entire country.

Prayagraj

Sangam

The sacred meeting point of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, where pilgrims gather to cleanse their sins. Come and Experience the serene energy at the confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers. It's a sight that evokes a deep sense of spirituality, where the merging waters symbolize the timeless wisdom and sacredness of the place.

Anand Bhawan

Step into Anand Bhawan and be transported to the era of India's freedom struggle. The museum breathes life into the stories of the Nehru-Gandhi family, leaving you inspired by their sacrifices and the journey towards independence.

Lucknow

Rumi Darwaza

The moment you visit here, you'd stand in awe before the magnificent Rumi Darwaza. This grand gateway showcases the architectural brilliance of Lucknow, immersing you in the city's rich history and cultural heritage.

Jama Masjid

Discover tranquility at the Jama Masjid, a beautiful mosque adorned with intricate designs. Step inside to feel the discipline, the aura, the beauty and the charm of the heritage this place offers!

Varanasi

Kashi Viswanath

You should definitely immerse yourself in the divine atmosphere of the Kashi Vishwanath Temple. The chants and incense fill the air, creating a deeply spiritual ambiance that connects you to the divine presence of Lord Shiva

Sarnath Museum

Wanna explore the treasures of Buddhism at the Sarnath Museum? Witness the ancient artifacts and sculptures that vividly depict Lord Buddha's teachings, offering a glimpse into the profound wisdom and cultural heritage of this sacred place.

Ayodhya

Hanuman Gadi

Experience the divine presence at Hanuman Garhi, a temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. From the elevated temple complex, you can witness a panoramic view of Ayodhya, feeling a surge of devotion and connecting with the legendary tales associated with Lord Hanuman.

Saryu River

Discover the serenity of the Saryu River as you walk along its banks. The gentle flow of the holy river creates a peaceful ambiance, allowing you to soak in the spiritual atmosphere and seek blessings amidst its sacred waters.

Mathura

Goverdhan Parvat

Visit the foot of Govardhan Parvat, a sacred hill associated with Lord Krishna's divine pastimes and come to feel the energy as you witness the grandeur of the hill, which is believed to have been lifted by Lord Krishna. It's a place where you can immerse yourself in the air, the wind and the captivating stories and legends of Lord Krishna.

Kanha Janmasthan

You can never have enough of the exquisite grandeur Lord Krishna has, another example to find is to visit the birthplace temple of Lord Krishna, known as Kanha Janmasthan. As you step into this sacred place, you can feel the divine presence and the profound spiritual significance it holds. It's a glimpse into the divine journey of Lord Krishna, inspiring devotion and reverence.
